VW-FEDA'2011 Call for Papers - deadline extended until Sept 5, 2011


Dear Colleague,

The deadline to submit a paper to the VIRTUAL WORLDWIDE FORUM ON ELECTRONIC DESIGN AUTOMATION 2011
(VW-FEDA'2011) has been extended until September 5, 2011.

Conference dates: November 28 - December 2, 2011

http://www.ieee.org/conferences_events/conferences/conferencedetails/index .html?Conf_ID=19543
http://www.feda.ecs.soton.ac.uk

Submissions are invited to VW-FEDA 2011, a high-quality worldwide virtual forum of postgraduate researchers working in Electronic Design Automation.  Papers can be submitted by current postgraduate students and recent PhD graduates.

The Forum is sponsored by the IEEE Council on Electronic Design Automation (CEDA). Participation is virtual, hence involves no travel, and offers participants the possibility of joining forum sessions on-line in live streamed video, live chat as well as off-line discussions with the authors and other delegates.

Presentation slides and video sessions will be archived and accessible on the VW FEDA website for 6 months after the event.

Submitted papers will be reviewed by world class academic and industrial researchers. The highest quality papers will be accepted for presentation at the Forum, published in the Forum Proceedings and indexed in the IEEE Digital Library.

Key dates:
===========
Paper submission deadline:  September 5, 2011 (Extended) 
Deadline for Industrial Fair session, special session and tutorial proposals: 29 August, 2011. 
Notification of acceptance: Friday, September 30, 2011.
Final versions of accepted papers and presenters' registration: October 31, 2011.
Presentation upload deadline: November 14, 2011.
